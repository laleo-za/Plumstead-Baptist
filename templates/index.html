Who we are

Our church exalts Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour and we proclaim this glorious Truth to all.

Acts 4:12  … there is salvation in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given among men by which we must be saved.

We’re a local Bible-believing church shaped by the Gospel and committed to worship, teaching, prayer, and community. On Sundays we gather as the redeemed people of God to pray, heard the Word preached, and worship our risen Saviour! Throughout the week we meet in smaller groups, serve our city, and encourage one another to follow Jesus in everyday life.
